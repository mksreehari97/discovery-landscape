Our Nursery

Supplying healthy, locally grown plants from our own Nurseries

Our nurseries in Al Bahyah and Al Rahbah, Abu Dhabi, are where we grow and maintain high-quality plants suited to the local climate. By managing our own plantation sites, Discovery Landscape ensures healthy growth, consistent quality, and a reliable supply for all landscaping needs.

Euphorbia

A drought-tolerant ornamental plant with unique textures, often used for borders and low-maintenance landscapes.

Category: Shrubs/Succulent

Height & Spread: 0.3m – 1.5m height & 0.3m – 1m spread

Origin: Africa / Madagascar

Climate: Arid and semi-arid, thrives in hot climates

Bougainvillea

A vibrant flowering climber known for its colorful bracts, widely used for walls, fences, and decorative landscaping.

Category: (Shrub) Climber

Height & Spread: Up to 1.5m (climber) & 0.5m – 1m spread

Origin: South America (Brazil)

Climate: Tropical and subtropical, highly drought-tolerant

Hibiscus

A popular flowering shrub with large, colorful blooms used in ornamental landscaping.

Category: Shrub

Height & Spread: 1 – 2m height & 1 – 2m spread

Origin: Asia

Climate: Tropical and subtropical

Hibiscus tiliaceus ‘Rubra’

A fast-growing ornamental shrub/tree with attractive foliage and hibiscus-like flowers.

Category: (Shrub) small tree

Height & Spread: 1.5 – 6m height & 1 – 4m spread

Origin: Tropical Asia

Climate: Coastal and tropical climates

Tecoma stans (Yellow Bells)

A fast-growing shrub with bright yellow trumpet-shaped flowers, commonly used in streetscapes and gardens.

Category: (Shrub) small tree

Height & Spread: 0.6cm – 3m height & 0.5 – 1m spread

Origin: Americas (Mexico to South America)

Climate: Arid to semi-arid, heat tolerant

Canna (Endeca)

A bold ornamental plant with large leaves and bright flowers, ideal for garden beds and water features.

Category: Flower

Height & Spread: 1 – 2.5m height & 0.5 – 1m spread

Origin: Tropical Americas

Climate: Warm tropical climates, prefers moist soil

Vinca rosea (Catharanthus roseus)

A small flowering plant with pink or white blooms, commonly used in beds and borders.

Category: (Flower) Ground cover

Height & Spread: 10cm – 20cm height & 10cm – 15cm spread

Origin: Madagascar

Climate: Tropical, thrives in hot and dry conditions

Category: Palm

Height & Spread: 2 – 4m height & 1.5 – 4m spread

Origin: Middle East / South Asia

Climate: Mediterranean, prefers mild winters and dry summers

Delonix regia (Flame Tree)

A striking ornamental tree with bright red flowers, creating a dramatic visual impact.

Category: Tree

Height & Spread: 2 – 6m height & 1.5 – 6m spread

Origin: Madagascar

Climate: Tropical, thrives in warm climates

Washingtonia (Fan Palm)

A tall ornamental palm widely used in avenues and landscaping for its elegant appearance.

Category: Palm

Height & Spread: 1.5 – 6m height & 1.5 – 3m spread

Origin: North America (California & Mexico)

Climate: Arid and semi-arid, highly heat tolerant

Plumeria(Frangipani)

A fragrant flowering tree commonly used in gardens and resorts for its tropical aesthetic.

Origin: Central America

Climate: Tropical and warm climates

Turnera (Turnera ulmifolia)

A small shrub with bright yellow flowers, suitable for ground cover and borders.

Category: Flower/ground cover

Height & Spread: 0.5 – 1m height & 0.5 – 1m spread

Origin: Central & South America

Climate: Tropical and subtropica

Prosopis cineraria (Ghaf Tree)

A native desert tree and national tree of the UAE, highly valued for its drought resistance.

Category: Tree

Height & Spread: 1.5– 10m height & 1.5 – 8m spread

Origin: Middle East / Indian subcontinent

Climate: Arid desert climate

Acacia arabica (Vachellia nilotica)

A hardy tree used for shade and soil improvement, known for its resilience in harsh environments.

Category: Tree

Height & Spread: 1.5 – 12m height & 1.5 – 18m spread

Origin: Africa / Indian subcontinent

Climate: Arid and semi-arid

Lycium shawii (Desert Thorn)

A hardy, thorny desert shrub commonly used in arid landscaping. It is well adapted to harsh environments and often used for natural fencing and habitat restoration.

Category: Shrub

Height & Spread: 1 – 3m height & 1 – 3m spread

Origin: Middle East, North Africa

Climate: Arid desert climate, highly drought-tolerant and thrives in hot conditions

Gazania uniflora

A low-growing, drought-tolerant flowering plant known for its bright daisy-like blooms. Commonly used in borders, beds, and ground cover applications for adding color to landscapes.

Category: Flower/Ground Cover

Height & Spread: 0.1 – 0.3m height & 0.3 – 0.6m spread

Origin: South Africa

Climate: Arid and semi-arid climates, thrives in full sun and well-drained soil

Bismarckia nobilis (Bismarck Palm)

A striking ornamental palm known for its large, fan-shaped silvery-blue leaves. Widely used as a focal point in landscaping due to its bold appearance and architectural form.

Category: Palm

Height & Spread: 5 – 14m height & 1.5 – 3m spread

Origin: Madagascar

Climate: Tropical to arid climates, highly tolerant to heat and drought, prefers full sun

Wodyetia bifurcata (Foxtail Palm)

An attractive ornamental palm known for its bushy, fox-tail-like fronds. Commonly used in avenues, residential landscapes, and feature planting for its elegant and tropical appearance.

Category: Palm

Height & Spread: 8 – 15m height & 3 – 5m spread

Origin: Australia (Queensland)

Climate: Tropical and subtropical climates, thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, moderately drought-tolerant once established

Trichilia emetica (Natal Mahogany)

A medium to large evergreen tree valued for its dense canopy and shade. Commonly used in landscaping for avenues and parks due to its attractive foliage and adaptability.

Category: Tree

Height & Spread: 2 –18m height & 6 – 12m spread

Origin: Africa

Climate: Tropical and subtropical climates, tolerant to heat and moderate drought, prefers well-drained soil

Spathodea campanulata (African Tulip Tree)

A fast-growing ornamental tree known for its large, bright orange-red, tulip-shaped flowers. Widely used in landscaping for its striking appearance and ability to provide shade.

Category: Tree

Height & Spread: 6 – 13m height & 3 – 5m spread

Origin: Tropical Africa

Climate: Tropical and subtropical climates, thrives in warm conditions with full sun, requires moderate water and well-drained soil
